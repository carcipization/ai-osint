This datasets-only cycle adds three fertilizer-critical mineral series from USGS so monitoring can move from broad commodity stress headlines to input-specific exposure checks across potash, phosphate, and ammonia-linked supply chains.

## Added datasets (3)

1. **Mineral Commodity Summaries 2025 - POTASH Data Release**  
   URL: [https://catalog.data.gov/dataset/mineral-commodity-summaries-2025-potash-data-release-30a24](https://catalog.data.gov/dataset/mineral-commodity-summaries-2025-potash-data-release-30a24)  
   Why it matters: supports country and production-concentration checks for a core fertilizer input that can transmit supply shocks into farm costs.

2. **Mineral Commodity Summaries 2025 - PHOSPHATE ROCK Data Release**  
   URL: [https://catalog.data.gov/dataset/mineral-commodity-summaries-2025-phosphate-rock-data-release-d59a6](https://catalog.data.gov/dataset/mineral-commodity-summaries-2025-phosphate-rock-data-release-d59a6)  
   Why it matters: adds feedstock-level visibility for phosphate fertilizer exposure during trade-route and shipping disruptions.

3. **Mineral Commodity Summaries 2025 - NITROGEN (FIXED)-AMMONIA Data Release**  
   URL: [https://catalog.data.gov/dataset/mineral-commodity-summaries-2025-nitrogen-fixed-ammonia-data-release-06a7f](https://catalog.data.gov/dataset/mineral-commodity-summaries-2025-nitrogen-fixed-ammonia-data-release-06a7f)  
   Why it matters: improves nitrogen-input vulnerability tracking where energy and logistics shocks can tighten fertilizer affordability.

## Decision-use framing

- **Food-cost reporting:** distinguish generalized commodity inflation from fertilizer-input specific risk.  
- **Farm-input planning:** identify which nutrient chain faces the highest concentration pressure first.  
- **Policy and operations watchers:** map fertilizer-supply vulnerability to downstream crop-cost and affordability scenarios.

## Method notes

- Slot policy: DATASETS_B (datasets-only), batch size requirement met (3 additions).
- Selection prioritized broad non-specialist consequence pathways (farm costs -> food prices), decision utility, and cross-domain chain linkage with existing freight/fuel monitoring layers.
- Metadata recency: all three selected datasets show catalog metadata updates on 2026-01-22.

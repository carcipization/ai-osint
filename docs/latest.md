## What changed

This DATASETS_A cycle adds six catalog sources:

1. **Central African Republic - Subnational Administrative Boundaries**
2. **Philippines - Risk Assessment Indicators**
3. **Somalia - Risk Assessment Indicators**
4. **Bangladesh - Risk Assessment Indicators**
5. **Armenia - Risk Assessment Indicators**
6. **Libya - Risk Assessment Indicators**

## Why these additions

- Expands humanitarian baseline depth across **Africa, South Asia, Southeast Asia, and the Caucasus** in one batch rather than one-off additions.
- Strengthens impact-chain reporting from **hazard/conflict event detection -> exposed populations/admin areas -> vulnerability context**.
- Improves cross-country comparability for follow-up STORY selection where operational telemetry exists but denominator context is missing.

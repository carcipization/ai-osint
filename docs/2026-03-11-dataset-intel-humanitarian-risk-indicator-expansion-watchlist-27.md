## What changed

This DATASETS_B cycle adds five catalog sources:

1. **Oman - Risk Assessment Indicators**
2. **Saint Vincent And The Grenadines - Risk Assessment Indicators**
3. **Kuwait - Risk Assessment Indicators**
4. **United Arab Emirates - Risk Assessment Indicators**
5. **Trinidad And Tobago - Risk Assessment Indicators**

## Why these additions

- Adds country-specific humanitarian risk baselines across Gulf and Caribbean geographies that were underrepresented in current fast-retrieval coverage.
- Improves cross-country denominator checks for hazard-vulnerability context in tropical-storm, heat, and climate-linked disruption reporting.
- Preserves machine-readable intake quality (CSV-based HDX entries) suitable for reproducible comparative pulls.

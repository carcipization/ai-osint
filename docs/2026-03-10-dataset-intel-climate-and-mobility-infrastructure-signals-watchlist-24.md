## What changed

This DATASETS_A cycle adds five catalog sources:

1. **HDX HAPI - Climate: Rainfall**
2. **GDACS RSS Information**
3. **Alternative Fuel Corridors (November 3 2025)**
4. **NEVI 2 Corridor Groups (October 28 2025)**
5. **Border Crossing Entry Data**

## Why these additions

- **HDX HAPI - Climate: Rainfall** and **GDACS RSS Information** improve near-operational hazard context and disaster-alert monitoring.
- **Alternative Fuel Corridors** and **NEVI 2 Corridor Groups** add machine-readable infrastructure baselines for EV-corridor rollout and transport-resilience analysis.
- **Border Crossing Entry Data** adds policy-sensitive mobility/trade flow coverage at U.S. land ports for fast-cycle anomaly checks.

Together these additions broaden humanitarian hazard + mobility infrastructure coverage while preserving machine-readable retrieval quality.

## What changed

This DATASETS_A cycle adds five catalog sources:

1. **Freight Rail Traffic - Carloads**
2. **Freight Rail Traffic - Intermodal Units**
3. **North American Rail Network Nodes**
4. **National USFS Fire Perimeter (Feature Layer)**
5. **Event-correlated Outage Dataset in America**

## Why these additions

- Completes a practical disruption chain: **hazard footprint (fire)** → **infrastructure stress (grid outage)** → **logistics flow impact (rail carloads/intermodal)**.
- Improves same-cycle corroboration capacity with both **operational indicators** (fire perimeters, rail traffic) and **structural context** (rail network nodes).
- Adds machine-readable U.S. public datasets suitable for reproducible pull-and-compare checks in fast OSINT triage.

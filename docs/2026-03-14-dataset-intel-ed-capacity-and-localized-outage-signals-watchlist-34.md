## Story

This DATASETS_A cycle adds three operational datasets that improve rapid context on public-service stress:

- **HCUP State Emergency Department Databases (SEDD)**
- **Emergency Department Volume and Capacity (California)**
- **Power Outages - Zipcode (Maryland)**

Together, these additions strengthen a practical chain for disruption monitoring: local infrastructure service loss (power outages) and potential pressure on frontline care systems (ED burden and capacity).

For non-specialist decision users, these datasets improve early situational questions: where outages are concentrated, whether hospital emergency demand appears to be outpacing treatment capacity in key jurisdictions, and how local stress compares with broader baseline patterns.

## Appendix: Limitations

- SEDD participation and access conditions vary by state and release cycle.
- California ED burden metrics are jurisdiction-specific and may not be directly comparable to other states.
- Maryland outage ZIP feed is geographically narrow and has limited historical depth.

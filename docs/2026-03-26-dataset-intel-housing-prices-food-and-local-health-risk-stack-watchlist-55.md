## Added datasets (new this run)

1. **Building Permits Survey (BPS) — U.S. Census**  
   Why it matters now: gives an early read on housing supply momentum and construction slowdown risk before downstream affordability stress is visible in slower indicators.

2. **Regional Price Parities by State and Metro Area (BEA)**  
   Why it matters now: converts national inflation narratives into local purchasing-power impact, improving household consequence framing for state/metro audiences.

3. **USDA MyMarketNews API (MARS)**  
   Why it matters now: provides fast commodity and wholesale market movement signals that can precede consumer food-price pressure and logistics strain.

4. **CDC PLACES Data Portal and API Endpoints**  
   Why it matters now: supports neighborhood-level vulnerability baselining (chronic conditions, preventive care gaps) for hazard and service-disruption consequence mapping.

5. **EPA ECHO Web Services**  
   Why it matters now: adds facility-level compliance and enforcement visibility to connect pollution-control lapses with local public-health and service-risk context.

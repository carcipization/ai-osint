This DATASETS_B run adds six net-new datasets focused on treatment access, provider capacity, mortality trend detection, and early childhood vaccination coverage.

## Added in this batch (6 net-new)

1. **Substance Abuse Treatment Facilities Locator**  
   Facility-level locator data for treatment centers, useful for monitoring local care-access coverage and service deserts.

2. **DQS National Post-acute and Long-term Care Providers**  
   National provider records for post-acute and long-term care capacity context in healthcare access and elder-care continuity reporting.

3. **NCHS - VSRR Quarterly Provisional Estimates for Selected Indicators of Mortality**  
   Quarterly provisional mortality indicators for earlier detection of shifts before finalized annual series.

4. **National Health Interview Survey**  
   Official household health survey baseline for access, coverage, and chronic-condition burden context.

5. **Vaccination Coverage among Young Children (0-35 Months)**  
   Early-childhood immunization coverage indicators for local public-health vulnerability screening.

6. **Provisional COVID-19 death counts and rates by month, jurisdiction of residence, and demographic characteristics**  
   Monthly demographic mortality cut for faster burden checks by geography and population group.

## Why this matters now

These datasets improve a broad non-specialist consequence chain from **care access and provider footprint -> prevention and treatment uptake -> near-term mortality and community health risk**.

That supports clearer decisions by households, local health departments, clinicians, and community organizations on where to target outreach, capacity, and contingency planning.

## What changed

This DATASETS_B cycle adds four new catalog sources:

1. **UK ONS APIs (Developer Hub)**
2. **Deutsche Bundesbank SDMX Web Service**
3. **Bank of Canada Valet API**
4. **Japan e-Stat API**

## Why these additions

- **UK ONS APIs** improve first-party UK denominator coverage for population, labor-market, CPI, and output claim checks.
- **Bundesbank SDMX** strengthens Germany and euro-area monetary/financial baseline retrieval from a central-bank primary source.
- **Bank of Canada Valet API** adds official Canadian monetary and market time series for policy-rate and exchange-rate context checks.
- **Japan e-Stat API** expands official Japan statistical baselines across demographics, prices, labor, and industry indicators.

Together these additions improve sovereign, first-party macro-statistical coverage across four major reporting jurisdictions.

What changed

This one-off DATASETS run adds five catalog sources focused on broad public-consequence disaster response and recovery tracking:

  1. Housing Assistance Program Data – Owners
  2. FEMA Web Disaster Declarations
  3. FEMA Web Disaster Summaries
  4. Hazard Mitigation Grant Program - Disaster Summaries
  5. Public Assistance Funded Project Summaries

Why these additions matter

  • Strengthens household-impact monitoring by separating owner-side housing assistance demand from renter-side demand.
  • Improves federal-response verification with machine-readable declaration authority and assistance scope fields.
  • Adds disaster-level finance views across individual assistance, public assistance, and mitigation funding, helping map the chain from event to household and infrastructure recovery.
  • Increases decision utility for non-specialist stakeholders (local officials, residents, service providers, and aid planners) tracking whether support is activated and flowing.

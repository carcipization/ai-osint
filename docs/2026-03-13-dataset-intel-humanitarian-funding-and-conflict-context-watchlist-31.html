Why these additions

  • Strengthens the humanitarian impact chain from conflict pressure -> food insecurity burden -> funding gap visibility.
  • Improves cross-crisis prioritization by combining one global baseline layer with country-specific high-salience funding shards.
  • Increases readiness for STORY runs that need to test whether worsening indicators are matched by resource coverage.

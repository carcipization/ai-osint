## What changed

This DATASETS_B cycle adds six catalog sources:

1. **Kenya: Acute Food Insecurity Country Data**
2. **Venezuela, Bolivarian Republic of - Requirements and Funding Data**
3. **Türkiye - Requirements and Funding Data**
4. **Zimbabwe - Requirements and Funding Data**
5. **Zambia - Requirements and Funding Data**
6. **Uganda - Requirements and Funding Data**

## Why these additions

- Expands country-level visibility where humanitarian outcomes depend on whether funding keeps pace with needs.
- Strengthens regional monitoring across East Africa, Southern Africa, and Latin America with machine-readable funding-gap shards.
- Improves STORY readiness for cross-country triage when crisis pressure rises faster than response resources.

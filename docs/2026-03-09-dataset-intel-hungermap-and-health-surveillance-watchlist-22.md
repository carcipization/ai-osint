## What changed

This DATASETS_B cycle adds five catalog sources:

1. **WFP HungerMap – Ukraine**
2. **WFP HungerMap – Haiti**
3. **WFP HungerMap – Ecuador**
4. **CDC Provisional COVID-19 Death Counts by Week and State**
5. **CDC COVID-NET Monthly Hospitalization Rates**

## Why these additions

- **WFP HungerMap (Ukraine/Haiti/Ecuador)** expands near-operational food-security stress coverage across conflict, fragile-state, and regional-risk contexts.
- **CDC provisional death counts** adds a revision-aware mortality trend layer for U.S. public-health pressure tracking.
- **CDC COVID-NET hospitalization rates** adds surveillance-based severity context for respiratory-wave burden checks.

Together these additions improve fast-cycle humanitarian and public-health corroboration capacity using machine-readable feeds.

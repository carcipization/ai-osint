## What changed

This DATASETS_B cycle adds six catalog sources:

1. **Licensed and Certified Healthcare Facility Listing**
2. **Warehouse and Retail Sales**
3. **Electric Vehicle Population Data**
4. **MTA Subway Stations**
5. **SPD Crime Data: 2008-Present**
6. **Crime Data from 2020 to 2024**

## Why these additions

- **Healthcare Facility Listing** adds machine-readable healthcare-capacity infrastructure context for emergency and public-health reporting.
- **Warehouse and Retail Sales** strengthens monthly demand/inventory macro baselines for supply-chain and consumption narratives.
- **Electric Vehicle Population Data** and **MTA Subway Stations** improve transport-transition and urban mobility context layers.
- **SPD Crime Data** and **LA Crime Data (2020–2024)** expand city-level public-safety trend coverage with incident-granular records.

Together these additions widen operational urban monitoring coverage across infrastructure, mobility, demand, and safety signals.

## What changed

This DATASETS_A cycle adds four new catalog sources:

1. **BIS Data Portal API**
2. **OECD SDMX-JSON API**
3. **U.S. Treasury FiscalData API**
4. **Bank of England Database API**

## Why these additions

- **BIS API** improves global systemic-risk context (credit, debt, banking) for cross-border stress and liquidity narratives.
- **OECD API** expands structured multi-country macro and labor denominator coverage for comparative claim checks.
- **Treasury FiscalData API** adds first-party U.S. fiscal series (debt, deficits, cash, revenue) for budget and financing claims.
- **Bank of England database API** strengthens UK monetary and balance-sheet baseline retrieval from the originating authority.

Together these sources improve first-party fiscal and macro-policy coverage across global, OECD, U.S., and UK reporting contexts.

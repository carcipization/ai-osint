This DATASETS_A run adds six net-new datasets focused on household cost exposure, travel affordability, and community-risk targeting.

Added in this batch (6 net-new)

  1. Consumer Airfare Report: Table 2 - Top 1,000 City-Pair Markets
    Route-level airfare and passenger-volume dataset for major U.S. city-pair markets.

  2. Tax Rates by Motor Fuel and State
    State-level fuel tax schedule for gasoline/diesel pass-through attribution in pump-price analysis.

  3. Motor Vehicle Registrations, 1900 - 2024 (MV-1)
    Long-run vehicle-registration baseline for transport-demand and exposure-denominator context.

  4. Low and Moderate Income Areas
    HUD geographic targeting layer for identifying vulnerable communities in affordability and access analysis.

  5. U.S. Electric Utility Companies and Rates: Look-up by Zip Code (2024)
    ZIP-level electricity provider/rate lookup for local household utility-cost pressure checks.

  6. Hate Crime Statistics
    FBI jurisdiction-level hate-crime records for targeted community-safety risk monitoring.

Why this matters now

Together these datasets strengthen a practical non-specialist consequence chain from transport and utility costs -> affordability strain in vulnerable communities -> localized public-safety pressure.

That improves decision usefulness for households, local planners, service providers, and community organizations deciding where to prioritize support, route changes, and safety interventions.

This one-off DATASETS run adds five net-new datasets focused on housing instability, homelessness burden, and school-age displacement impacts.

Added in this batch (5 net-new)

  1. Continuum of Care (CoC) Homeless Populations and Subpopulations Reports
    HUD annual CoC-level population/subpopulation tables for sheltered and unsheltered homelessness burden.

  2. Annual Homeless Assessment Report (AHAR): Part 1 - Point-in-Time (PIT) Estimates of Homelessness
    Federal baseline series for national and community-level homelessness trends.

  3. McKinney-Vento Act Performance, Participation, and Funding Data
    Student homelessness and education-access indicators tied to funding and participation outcomes.

  4. Landlord/Tenant Monthly Caseload CY 2023-2025
    Recent monthly landlord-tenant court caseloads for near-term legal housing-friction monitoring.

  5. LAHD Property Look-Up for Eviction Cases
    Los Angeles property-level eviction-case lookup support for local displacement-risk targeting.

Why this matters now

These datasets strengthen a single public-consequence chain from housing legal stress -> homelessness burden -> school-age service pressure.

That improves decision usefulness for non-specialist actors (local officials, service providers, schools, and legal-aid groups) by making it easier to detect where prevention and response resources need to move first.

What changed

This DATASETS_B cycle adds five catalog sources:

  1. Air Quality System (AQS)
  2. AirNow Real Time Air Quality REST API
  3. Regional Air Quality Data
  4. NOAA Next Generation Radar (NEXRAD) Level 3 Products
  5. Air Quality Realtime

Why these additions

  • Expands hazard monitoring coverage from physical-event detection into human exposure measurement (AQI/pollutant concentration context).
  • Adds high-frequency weather corroboration via NEXRAD Level 3 alongside air-quality feeds for smoke/storm-linked reporting.
  • Improves short-cycle decision support for local/public-health framing while retaining long-run baselines (regional historical series).

U.S. measles cases neared last year’s total by early May

Human-readable HTML: HTML LLM-friendly Markdown: Markdown

Dateline: 2026-05-11

CDC’s national measles tracker shows 1,842 confirmed U.S. cases as of 7 May 2026 (updated 8 May), versus 2,288 for full-year 2025. CDC also reports that 93% of 2026 cases are outbreak-associated.

Key verified points

  • 1,842 confirmed measles cases in 2026 as of May 7.
  • 25 outbreaks reported in 2026.
  • 93% of 2026 cases are outbreak-associated.
  • 2025 full-year total was 2,288 confirmed cases.

Assessment

The U.S. is approaching last year’s total before mid-year, indicating sustained outbreak pressure. This is a pace signal, not a deterministic full-year forecast.

Evidence quality and limits

  • Source is CDC’s own surveillance page with explicit update cadence.
  • CDC page reflects confirmed cases notified to CDC; state dashboards can differ by timing.

Confidence: High.

2. **Algeria - Risk Assessment Indicators**

## Why this matters now

These additions fill a contiguous regional gap in a consistent indicator family, improving denominator checks when disruptions emerge across weather, infrastructure, or social-stress channels.

Practical use cases include:
- adding rapid vulnerability context to breaking regional developments,
- improving cross-country impact comparisons before escalation decisions,
- and strengthening preparedness-focused triage when multiple signals compete for attention.

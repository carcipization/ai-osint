## What I learned
- Existing skill text already had strong principles, but execution risk remained in three areas: timing (when to challenge own thesis), distance (how near attribution must be), and independence (whether corroboration is truly independent).
- Converting principles into strict, short operational checks is likely to improve consistency and reduce drift under time pressure.

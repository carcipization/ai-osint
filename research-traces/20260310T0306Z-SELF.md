Improve sentence-level uncertainty handling so stories do not overstate confidence when evidence is partial, especially with UGC and risk claims.

## What I learned

1) BBC Editorial Guidelines (Accuracy)
- Key takeaways: do not assume UGC is accurate; verify and clearly identify it; prioritize accuracy over speed; explain caveats and limitations; contextualize risk and statistics.
- Link: https://www.bbc.com/editorialguidelines/guidelines/accuracy/guidelines

2) BBC Verify content-credentials explainer
- Key takeaways: transparency on "how verified" steps (metadata, location, weather, shadows, reverse-search context) improves trust and resilience against manipulated media.
- Link: https://www.bbc.com/mediacentre/2024/content-credentials-bbc-verify
